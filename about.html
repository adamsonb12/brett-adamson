Marriage is the Best

My beautiful wife Cassie and I met at her sisters wedding. I was the best man and she was the maid of honor. Have you ever heard a better story? I got her number that same night, and we started dating just 13 days later. We got married in 2016, making me the luckiest man on earth.

Cassie and I love be adventurous, our first date was a long hike to explore caves! We love to go watch BYU sports, cook, work out, play volleyball, snowboard, climb mountains, and do lots of homework together.

I Live to Play Sports

I'm ridiculously competitive, especially in football and basketball. I have played football and basketball since I was born, and still play frequently. There are always pick up games at BYU, and my flag football team is ranked #1 in the whole school!

I was an all-state strong safety and tight end in highschool, and continue to play football any chance I get. I also am BYU football's biggest fan. I go absolutely nuts watching those games!

Although football and basketball are my preferred sports to play, I have played most every sport I know. I play a variety of other sports, in fact, I'll play almost anything if I have the chance. Lately, I've been playing a lot of soccer, ultimate frisbee, and volleyball.
