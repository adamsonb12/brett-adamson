Life is Good

Hi I'm Brett! I'm married to Cassie, the most wonderful woman in the world. We met at her older sisters wedding, where she was the maid of honor and I was the best man. It was love at first sight. I got her number that day, asked her out, and we started dating in less than two weeks. Life has been absolutely incredible since Cassie came into my life.

We live in Utah, we both are attending Brigham Young University and we are huge Cougar fans! I'm studying Information Systems in BYU's business school. I love what I learn and I love to code, but Cassie and I love to do a lot of other things too.

"Life is and Adventure"

We are suppposed to have fun! Cass and I spend as much time outside as we can. We love to go hiking, backpacking, running, and we even just love to go outside to read or do homework. We can see the towering Rocky mountains outside our apartment windows and take frequent drive up the canyons to go on short hikes and adore stunning scenic views showing the whole Salt Lake Valley.

I love Sports

I'm ridiculously competitive, especially in football and basketball. I have played football and basketball since I was born, and still play frequently. There are always pick up games at BYU, and my flag football team is ranked #1 in the whole school!

I was an all-state strong safety and tight end in highschool, and continue to play football any chance I get. I also am BYU football's biggest fan. I go absolutely nuts watching those games!

Although football and basketball are my preferred sports to play, I have played most every sport I know. I play a variety of other sports, in fact, I'll play almost anything if I have the chance. Lately, I've been playing a lot of soccer, ultimate frisbee, and Cassie and I play volleyball together every chance we get.
